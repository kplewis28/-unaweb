A thread between worlds

ÚNA weaves ancestral cultures and contemporary thought into experiences
and immersive spaces that give more than they take.
Our work centres and collaborates with those who protect cultural and
traditional ecological knowledge, and cultivates a global community working
toward a thriving and balanced planet.

The experience

Cultural
Immersion

ÚNA opens rare access to living cultures that have tended the ‘Heart of the World’ for centuries in the Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta, Colombia. Each gathering unfolds over five days on ancestral land, hosted and led by Indigenous women knowledge keepers who invite a select circle into their lands and sacred sites.

Beyond a curated experience, ÚNA creates an encounter of worlds, ways of knowing, being, and stewarding. A space for those willing to sit with the discomfort, to unlearn what dominant cultures have taught, to engage cosmologically with the natural world around in ways that may change everything.

The experience

Small
Circle

A maximum of 15 selected participants are invited into each gathering. Through ceremony, land-based learning, and deep listening, ÚNA weaves participants with territory, ancestral wisdom, and each other. They arrive as guests, yet leave as kin. We become ÚNA.

The experience

Roots

Every gathering leaves something behind. A positive impact where land and place are left in a better condition than found. Women’s houses, herbal and food gardens, communal infrastructure towards Indigenous sovereignty.
